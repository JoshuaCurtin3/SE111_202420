About Saving Private Ryan

This movie follows a group of U.S. soldiers who land in the beaches of Normandy. After successfully storming the beach, Captain Miller, Tom Hanks, and his crew were assigned to retrieve a paratrooper whose 3 other brothers have killed in action. As you follow these men throughout the war, you see the different conflicts they run into and how they handle them. Saving Private Ryan is a cinematic masterpiece and everyone should take some time to watch this movie.
