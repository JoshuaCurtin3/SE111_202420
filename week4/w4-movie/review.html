ABOUT CAST REVIEW

REVIEW

Joshua Curtin | 2/5/2024

Personally, I believe this is one of the greatest films ever made. The first time I watched Saving Private Ryan I remember feeling so invested into the movie. Right from the first 10 minutes you see how brutal and disguisting war is, and how these people just fight through it.

I love movies with Matt Damon, but Tom Hanks really made me love this movie. His character is so unique and makes you wonder what it must be like for him. Steven Spielburg, one of the most famous directors ever, made one of, if not the best, war movies ever.
